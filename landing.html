Layout Engines

Five algorithmic styles.
Infinite creative possibilities.

Each layout engine uses a unique procedural algorithm driven by a deterministic seed. Same seed = same poster, every time. Change the seed = an entirely new composition.

🌀

Vortex

Logarithmic spirals with particle trails create hypnotic vortex patterns. Particles spiral outward from the center, leaving elegant trails that evoke cosmic whirlpools and mathematical beauty.

Logarithmic Spiral + Particle Trail
🔷

Mondrian

Recursive rectangle subdivision inspired by De Stijl art movement. Clean geometric compositions with bold color blocks, perfect for gallery walls, posters, and modern graphic design.

Recursive Subdivision
🌌

Galaxy

Gravity simulation with particle orbits generates nebula-like compositions. Thousands of particles interact through simulated gravity, forming spiral arms, star clusters, and cosmic dust clouds.

Gravity Simulation + Orbits
💠

Kaleidoscope

N-fold rotational symmetry creates mesmerizing mandala-like patterns. Shapes are mirrored and rotated around the center, producing sacred geometry and intricate decorative compositions.

Rotational N-fold Symmetry
🌊

Fluid

Multi-layer Perlin noise flow fields produce organic, smoke-like compositions. Particles follow smooth vector fields, creating atmospheric gradients that evoke liquid, smoke, and wind patterns.

Perlin Noise Flow Field
